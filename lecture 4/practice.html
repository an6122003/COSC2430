Product

With the current COVID situation where people are concerned about getting infected from contacting the outer source, the level of sensitivity for sanitization is very high. As important as it is to stay safe from germs, being hydrated is also as important. Our team, AutoWater, has decided to figure out a system that can aid us to be both hydrated and healthy from infections. Introducing the auto water filling dispenser machine.

Our machine can fill a standardized cup that can hold at least 300 ml of liquid. Just place a cup into the filling platform, The Auto Water Dispenser will detect and fill it with liquid until 300ml.

Our Main Features

Standardized cup

Automatic system

Contact-free

Progress

Week 1-4

Discussing and deciding our product idea for the project. Set the topic as an auto water dispenser. Discussing about the procedures and setting the overall timeline of the procedures.

Week 5-8

Planning out the designs and parts to be used. The layout of the parts and machine general shape is determined. After the designs are all done, we start ordering all the electronics to test them.

Week 9-12

The electronics have all been thoroughly tested and work find so we started designing and building the enclosure and user interface. All the enclosure parts are then either 3d printed or CNC laser cut. Then we assemble everything together to test it as a whole system.
